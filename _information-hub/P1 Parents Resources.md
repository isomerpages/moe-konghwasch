<h3>How can I prepare my child for Primary 1?</h3>
</blockquote>
<p>In primary school, your child will be equipped with skills to:</p>
<ul>
<li>
<p>adjust to a larger learning environment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>interact with more peers and teachers</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>adapt to longer school hours&nbsp;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>become more independent and responsible</p>
