### Project Work

Project Work aims to provide students with an inter-disciplinary learning experience that encourages them to break away from the compartmentalisation of disciplines, thus equipping them with the necessary competencies of the 21st Century while making learning engaging and authentic. In groups, students brainstorm for ideas using SCAMPER and visible thinking tools before designing their Mathematics or Science toys.
