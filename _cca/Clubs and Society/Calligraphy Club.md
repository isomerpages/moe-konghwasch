The main objective of the Calligraphy Club is to instill knowledge of the basic skills and techniques in Chinese calligraphy. In addition, the Club aims to expose students to Chinese history and culture and, ultimately, inculcate in them school values and a love for the Chinese Language. Students will learn Chinese New Year couplets (春联) during Chinese New Year period. They will also have the opportunity to participate in SYF programme.
